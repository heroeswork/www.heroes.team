Prema Scholarship Fund

Prema, Dr. Morrison, aka "The Heart Lady" was a Wraparound champion that changed the lives of youth, families, staff, and communities across California.

Today, we continue to celebrate the life of our friend, colleague, and mentor by supporting others in their journey of growth and discovery. Prema believed in the power of knowledge and that high quality training shouldn't be a luxury that only priviledged programs receive. Through this scholarship we try to spread her "love" through equitable access to trainings for everyone. If funding is an obstacle for you, apply for assistance through this scholarship program created in her name.

If, like Prema, you also believe good training should not be a luxury, please consider donating to the fund to help others.
